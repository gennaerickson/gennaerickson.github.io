Summer 2014 Class Schedule — Utah Valley University Wasatch Campus

Each semester, UVU Wasatch has a flyer created with their available upcoming classes. I’ve designed these flyers for them for five semesters, each semester with a different theme and design. The challenge with these flyers is to fit a lot of information on just an 8.5x11 piece of paper and still make them visually interesting.
