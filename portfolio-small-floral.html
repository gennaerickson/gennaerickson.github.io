Floral Fabric Print — Ooh La Leggings

Ooh La Leggings asked me to design a series of fabric prints for their new line of leggings. I worked with the owner to decide what the prints should look like and what we thought popular colors and patterns would be. Once the designs were approved I then worked with the owner and the vendor throughout the printing process.
