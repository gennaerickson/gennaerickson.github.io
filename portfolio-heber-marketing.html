Marketing Collateral Suite — Heber Valley Tourism & Economic Development

While working at the Heber Valley tourism department, I was part of a rebranding campaign for the valley. We used a design firm to create a new logo and then, using that as a basis for our style moving forward, I created a suite of marketing materials, including digital and print ads, trade show booth, popup banners, magazine spreads and weekly tourism columns for print and the web. In addition to the design work, I also wrote much of the copy for these pieces.
